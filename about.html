About PassX

Welcome to PassX your personal password manager. As our online lives grow increasingly complex, it's important to keep our credentials safe and organized. All passwords are encrypted, and with PassX you will never forget your passwords again!

Our Mission

PassX is a step towards simple and hassle-free password management. So, we set out to make a simple tool that will allow you to keep all your passwords and manage them in one safe place. We aim to improve your digital security with convenience and ease of use. It's Fully Free and Open-Source, and you can even run it on your own browser locally.(Check the Github Repo by Clicking on the GitHub Icon in the Header)

Key Features

  1. Store and manage your passwords securely
  2. Quickly create, remove, and change your passwords
  3. One-click copy passwords to clipboard
  4. Friendly interface and intuitive design
  5. Saved to Your Local Storage for Extra Privacy
  6. Fully Free and Open-Source
